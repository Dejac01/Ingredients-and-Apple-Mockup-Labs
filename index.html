Lobster Mac n Cheese

This is how you would make lobster Mac n Cheese.

ingredients :

  • Lobster Meat
  • Pasta Noodles
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Your Choice of Seasoning
  • Gruyere Cheese
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Preheat Oven to 375
  • Drizzle cooking oil into a pot of boiling salted water
  • Add Pasta to pot and cook for 6-8 minutes or until soft
  • Drain noodles after cooking
  • Heat milk in microwave for warm temperature
  • Melt 6 tablespoons of butter and add flower
  • Cook mixed butter and flower while stiring on low for 2-3 minutes
  • Add the warm milk and stir until thick and smooth
  • Add cheese and your choice of seasoning
  • Add in cooked pasta and lobster meat and mix well
  • Place the mixture in a glass pan
  • Melt remaining butter and combine with fresh bread crumbs
  • Sprnkle bread crumb mixture on top of macaroni noodles
  • Place pan in oven and bake for 35 minutes
  • Enjoy!
